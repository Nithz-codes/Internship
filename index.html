01 / About

A consulting firm that also builds the thing it recommends

idThirdeye Technology Solutions (iTTS) is a Chennai-based technology and consulting company working across digital marketing, education, business operations, and talent. The team is small enough to stay hands-on and experienced enough to have done this before — at scale, for both startups and established businesses.

Leadership spans strategy, delivery, and industry partnerships — CEO Indra Devi sets direction, Project Manager Leela Devi runs delivery, and Industry Collaboration Advisor Adi Arunachalam keeps the company connected to where the market is actually heading.
